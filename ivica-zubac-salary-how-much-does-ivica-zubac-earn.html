Ivica Zubac Salary: How much does Ivica Zubac earn?

Ivica Zubac is a Croatian professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Clippers of the National Basketball Association. He was drafted by the Los Angeles Lakers in the second round of the 2016 NBA draft. He played for the Lakers until the 2019 trade deadline when he was traded to the Clippers.

How much does Ivica Zubac earn?

In July 2019 the center signed a contract with the Los Angeles Clippers that nets him a massive salary of 7 Million Dollars (5.9 Million Euros) per year. We have crunched the numbers, this means he earns $19,178 (€16,152) per day and $799 (€673) per hour!

Zubac came through the youth system of Cibona and then played for Zrinjevac in the second Croatian division during the 2013–14 season. He made the roster of Cibona’s men’s team the following season, becoming a part of a group of prospects including Nik Slavica and Ante Žižić after the departure of Dario Šarić.
