Clint Howard Net Worth

Clint Howard has a net worth of $4 million, as of 2021. He has accumulated his wealth through his career as an actor, director and producer in Hollywood. As for his salary, it is believed to be around $200,000 per year.

Early Life and Education

Born on April 20, 1959, in Burbank, California, Clint Howard was the younger brother of the famous actor and director, Ron Howard. He began his acting career at the age of two, appearing in The Twilight Zone TV series, alongside his brother. As a child, he attended the Robert Louis Stevenson Elementary in Burbank and then studied at John Burroughs High School.

Career

Howard’s career spans over five decades, starting with his debut role in The Twilight Zone. He is best known for his appearances in films such as Apollo 13, The Waterboy, and Austin Powers: International Man of Mystery. He appeared in several episodes of The Andy Griffith Show, and he also had roles in many other TV shows, including Star Trek, Arrested Development, and Seinfeld.

In addition to his acting career, Howard has also directed and produced several films such as The Paper Brigade, which he directed in 1997, and he produced When Zachary Beaver Came to Town in 2003. He has contributed his voice to several video game characters, including Kid in Minecraft: Story Mode.

Personal Life

Howard has been married to his longtime girlfriend, Melanie Sorich, since 1995. The couple has one daughter named Chloe. They run a post-production company, Imagine Entertainment.

Real Estate

Howard owns a house in Tujunga, Los Angeles County, California, worth around $600,000. The property is spread over 2,000 square feet.

Legacy

Clint Howard is an icon in the entertainment industry, having appeared in over 250 TV shows and films. He has received recognition from the industry, having won the Lifetime Achievement Award at the Burbank International Film Festival in 2019.
