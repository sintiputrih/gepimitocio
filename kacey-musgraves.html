Kacey Musgraves News - Us Weekly

Name:Kacey Musgraves

Hometown:Golden, Texas

Birthday:August 21, 1988

About

Hailing from Golden, Texas, Kacey Musgraves rose to fame in 2007 after appearing on Nashville Star, placing seventh overall. Musgraves has since released four feature-length albums, which earned her six Grammy Awards and an additional seven nominations. She starred in the Kacey Musgraves Christmas Show special for Amazon Prime Video in November 2019. Her fourth album, Star-Crossed, was released in September 2021.

The “Breadwinner” songstress married Ruston Kelly in October 2017 after one year of dating. The twosome announced their separation in July 2020. After their divorce was finalized that September, Musgraves was briefly linked to Dr. Gerald Onuoua in April 2021. She later moved on with poet Cole Schafer, confirming their relationship in August 2021.
