Complete Winners List Celeb Secrets

The time has finally come — the Kids’ Choice Awards have landed in Los Angeles!

This evening, all of the biggest stars are coming together for the slimiest event of the year, the 2019 Kids’ Choice Awards. 

2019 Kids’ Choice Awards: Orange Carpet Arrivals

Airing live from the USC Galen Center in Los Angeles, the television event will feature slimetastic moments from host DJ Khaled and Nickelodeon stars including JoJo Siwa, Jace Norman and Riele Downs (Henry Danger), Ryan of Ryan ToysReview (Ryan’s Mystery Playdate), Scarlet Spencer and Dallas Dupree Young (Cousins For Life) and Owen Joyner, Daniella Perkins and Lilimar (Knight Squad), and a show-stopping performance from Migos featuring DJ Mustard.

Ariana Grande, Adam Sandler, Chris Pratt, Jason Sudeikis, Noah Centineo, Lana Condor, Joey King, Liza Koshy, Paris Berelc, Caleb McLaughlin, Kiernan Shipka, Ally Brooke, Josh Peck, Lilly Singh and David Dobrik, along with cast members from Disney’s Aladdin (Will Smith, Mena Massoud and Naomi Scott), Shazam! (Zachary Levi, Asher Angel and Jack Dylan Grazer) and Dora and the Lost City of Gold (Isabela Moner and Eugenio Derbez). are all confirmed to make appearances at the show tonight, but we’re almost positive that more surprises will come throughout the evening.

How were our predictions? Compare the lists here.

Take a look at the full winners’ list below, as it will be updated constantly throughout the day as new winners are announced!

Hosted by DJ Khaled, the 2019 Kids’ Choice Awards will air tonight, March 23rd @ 8PM ET/PT on Nickelodeon.
