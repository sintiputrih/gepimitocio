Who Are Alan Williams Siblings?: Born on November 4, 1969, Alan Williams is a former American football coach.

He’s also a former player who was previously the defensive coordinator for the Chicago Bears of the National Football League.

As a former player, Between 1988 and 1991, Williams had his college career as a running back at William & Mary

He finished his playing career at William & Mary with 1,220 rushing yards and 15 rushing touchdowns, while he totaled 131 receptions for 1,331 yards with 7 scores.

As a coach, Williams was an assistant coach from 1992-95 for Norview High School, where he also coached track and field.

He returned to William & Mary as a coach in 1996, coaching running backs from 1996–97 and defensive backs from 1998–2000.

Williams got his first NFL coaching experience coaching under Tony Dungy in the Tampa Bay Buccaneers organization in 2001

In 2002, he followed Tony Dungy to the Indianapolis Colts where he served as the defensive backs coach for the Colts from 2002 to 2011.

With the Colts, he won Super Bowl XLI against the Chicago Bears. In January 2012, he was hired to be the defensive coordinator of the Minnesota Vikings.

In January 2014, Williams joined the Detroit Lions defensive coaching staff and in February 2018, he returned to the Indianapolis Colts as the defensive backs/safeties coach.

On February 2, 2022, Williams became the defensive coordinator for the Chicago Bears of the National Football League.

In September 2023, the Bears defensive coordinator hit the headlines following his resignation, the team announced on Wednesday, September 20.

Williams stepped away from the team last week, citing personal issues. “I am taking a step back to take care of my health and family.

I appreciate the opportunity to work with the Chicago Bears, a storied NFL franchise with a rich history,” Williams said in a statement.
