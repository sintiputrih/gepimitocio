Trey McBride contract, salary and net worth explored

On November 22, 1999, Trey McBride, a tight end for the Arizona Cardinals of the National Football League, was born. He participated in college football for Colorado State, where he was named an unquestioned All-American and received the John Mackey Award in 2021. McBride was taken by the Cardinals in the second round of the 2022 NFL Draft.

McBride was born and raised in Fort Morgan, Colorado, where he also attended Fort Morgan High School and participated in football, basketball, and baseball. He was named Class 3A All-Colorado during his junior season, which included 32 catches for 751 yards and 11 touchdowns.

As a senior, he was once again named to the All-Colorado team after rushing for 226 yards, receiving 30 passes for 450 yards, and scoring four touchdowns in all. He also had 40 defensive tackles and one sack. In addition to holding the school records for lifetime home runs and RBIs in baseball, McBride also retains the marks for career points scored in basketball.
