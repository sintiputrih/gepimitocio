Ruth Bell - Age, Family, Bio

Model who shaved her head to book a campaign with David Sims and McQueen. She was photographed by Hedi Slimane for Saint Laurent. She has over 60,000 followers on Instagram.   

Before Fame

Her twin sister May Bell is also a model. Her mother dressed them up differently throughout their childhood.   

Trivia

She and her sister found their way into modeling when a scout approached them in Paris. It took them a year to reach out to the scout. 

Family Life

She grew up in Dover, a part of Kent, just South of London. 

Associated With

She has an obsession with Courtney Love.
