Who is Mike Zito:

Mike Zito is a famous Guitarist. He was born on November 19, 1970 and his birthplace is Missouri. Mike is originated from United States. He has collaborated with percussionist Michito Sanchez, who has worked as a session musican for artists like Nelly Furtado.
