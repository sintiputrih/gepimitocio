How Did Kevin Hart's Net Worth Grow By $250 Million In One Year?

Highlights

  • Kevin Hart's net worth has skyrocketed from $200 million in 2022 to an estimated $450 million in 2023, and he is on track to become a billionaire soon.
  • Hart's success comes from his talent, hard work, and strategic business ventures, including combining his entertainment enterprises to create Hartbeat Productions and securing a $100 million investment from Abry Partners.
  • In addition to his entertainment career, Kevin Hart has lucrative partnerships with brands like JP Morgan Bank, Sam's Club, and DraftKings, as well as his own tequila company and plant-based fast-food chain.

Kevin Hart is not just a famously hilarious entertainer; he is also insanely wealthy! While he was rich in 2022, he took a massive ride up the financial ladder this past year. In recent years, Kevin Hart has been extremely busy, taking on various roles such as comedian, movie star, producer, and commercial spokesperson.

His immense success and wealth are a direct result of his exceptional talent and good old-fashioned hard work. Exactly how much money does Kevin Hart have? More than enough to buy himself a pet eagle and Nick Cannon a condom vending machine.

What Is Kevin Hart's Net Worth?

As of 2022, many sources reported Kevin Hart's net worth was $200 million; currently, the approximate amount is around $450 million. He is nowhere near plateauing, either. In fact, according to The Strive, considering how fast his net worth has grown since 2010 (he has gone from a net worth of $9 million to $450 million in just 12 short years), he will be a billionaire soon.

Kevin Hart's career started as a comedian, and like several others comedians, his career then progressed into acting. Some other famous comedians who found great success as actors include, Eddie Murphy with a net worth of $200 million, Jim Carrey with a net worth of $180 million and Steve Carell with a net worth of $80 million.

Kevin Hart's net worth towers above the rest. In fact, last year he was named The Hollywood Reporters' celebrity entrepreneur of the year.

What Happened With Kevin Hart's Net Worth?

So, what exactly happened that made Kevin Hart's net worth spike? For that, we have to go back just over a decade. Kevin Hart started out going on comedy tour after comedy tour, and he was selling out every show. These were not little comedy clubs, mind you; these were big, stadium-sized arenas. He was also starring in movies that topped the box office and started releasing his Netflix comedy specials.

  • I’m a Grown Little Man (2009)
  • Kevin Hart: What Now? (2016)
  • Kevin Hart’s Guide to Black History (2019)
  • Kevin Hart: Irresponsible (2019)
  • Best of Stand-Up 2020 (2020)

In today's world of million-dollar Instagram posts, being famous for the right things can get you into a lot of places you might not have had the option to before. As for Kevin Hart, he has mastered the art of monetizing his fame. He has strategically built a multifaceted business empire. This powerhouse trickles down through a wide range of industries too. Film and television, alcohol, biotech and financial tech, sportswear, and more. But one of his ventures in particular has really paid off for him recently.

Kevin Hart made a significant move by combining his two entertainment enterprises, Hartbeat Productions and Laugh Out Loud, to create a fresh and unified entity known as Hartbeat. To propel its expansion, the company secured a substantial investment of $100 million from Abry Partners, which is a private equity firm.

This strategic decision led to the company's rapid expansion, establishing itself as one of the most rapidly growing and highly sought-after creative powerhouses for various platforms, streaming services, and brands. In the year 2022, Hartbeat successfully launched over 20 projects, and in 2023, it has already released 18 projects. Furthermore, an impressive lineup of more than 70 projects is currently in development, proving the company's persistent commitment to producing compelling content and making a significant impact in the entertainment industry.

Hartbeat has significantly added to Kevin Hart's net worth, and its own. The company is currently worth a massive $650 million.

What Other Projects Increased Kevin Hart's Net Worth?

One thing you should know about Kevin Hart is that while he is the busiest man in Hollywood, he isn't just doing it for the money. Hart wholeheartedly believes in every product he reps. In an interview with Forbes, he said, "To sit here and say money doesn't play a part in some deals, you wouldn't be telling the truth. But more important for me is: What if this brand becomes the biggest ever? What if the campaign becomes the most talked-about thing ever? My name and my brand would be attached to that."

Some of those brands include JP Morgan Bank, Sam's Club, and DraftKings. There are no exact numbers on the financials of these partnerships, but to say a couple million would not be exaggerating. Not only that, but Hart started his own brand of tequila. If that sounds familiar, it is because one of his best friends, Dwayne Johnson, also has a tequila company in direct competition.

Several alcohol companies had contacted Hart over the years, but he didn't decide until he met with Juan Domingo Beckmann, an 11th-generation tequila maker, and together they created Gran Coramino.

Kevin Hart didn't stop there; now that he had a drink, he needed food to go with it. Hart House, his plant-based fast-food chain, opened its first location in Los Angeles' Westchester neighborhood. He also made three movies in 2022. Me Time, The Man from Toronto, and the DC League of Super Pets. The first two mentioned are Netflix films, in which The Vulture reports Kevin Hart has an eight-figure deal. Basically, he makes around $20 million per movie. Yes, please!

Yes, it seems Kevin Hart can do anything. While we look forward to seeing what the future holds for him, you can currently find him on his "2023 Reality Check" comedy tour.
