Daxton Hill is an American football safety for the Cincinnati Bengals of the National Football League (NFL).

Hill played college football at Michigan, where as a true freshman in 2019, he played in 13 games with three starts and had 36 tackles and one interception.

As a sophomore in 2020, Hill started six games, recording 46 tackles and one interception.

Hill returned to Michigan as a starter in 2021 and received 1st team all Big Ten honors.

On January 6, 2022, Hill declared for the 2022 NFL Draft.
