Piers Morgan: Net Worth, Age, Wife, Biography, Children, Parents, and Latest Updates


Discover all the latest on Piers Morgan, including his net worth, age, wife, biography, children, and parents.

Piers Morgan has made a lasting impression in the British entertainment industry with his roles as a broadcaster, journalist, and television personality. He is recognized for his appearances on popular shows like “America’s Got Talent” and “Good Morning Britain,” where his engaging personality and sharp wit have captivated audiences.

His ability to connect with viewers and offer insightful commentary on current events has solidified his status as a household name in the entertainment industry.

Piers attributes his success in entertainment to a blend of charm, wit, and a profound understanding of current affairs and politics. His fearless journalism and candid viewpoints on diverse subjects have made him a renowned figure.

Profile Summary/Wiki

Piers Morgan Biography

Piers Morgan, an English broadcaster, journalist, and television personality, is known for his outspoken and often controversial opinions. Since the start of his media career in 1988 with “The Sun,” he has continued to rise to new heights, leveraging his attractive, charismatic, and eloquent personality.

Piers Morgan Family Background

Piers Morgan’s birth name was Piers Stefan Pughe-Morgan O’Meara. His father, Vincent Eamonn O’Meara, was an Irish dentist, and his mother, Gabrielle Georgina Sybille, was English. His father’s passing when he was 11 years old led to his mother’s remarriage to Glynne Pughe-Morgan, and Piers subsequently took on his stepfather’s surname.

Piers Morgan Siblings

Piers Morgan’s older brother, Jeremy Morgan, shares the same biological father, Vincent, who passed away when Piers was 11. Afterwards, Piers took on the surname Morgan from his stepfather, Glynne Morgan.

Piers Morgan Education

For his secondary education, Piers attended Chailey School and then proceeded to the Priory School for his sixth form. His journalism studies took place at Harlow College.

Piers Morgan Age

Piers Morgan was born on March 30, 1965, in Surrey, England, making him 57 years old today. Despite his late 50s, Piers remains highly active and influential within the media industry.

Piers Morgan Ethnicity

Piers Morgan’s ethnicity encompasses Irish, Scottish, English, and Portuguese roots.

Piers Morgan Net Worth

What is Piers Morgan’s Net Worth? With reports suggesting earnings of $22 million in a single year during his career, his net worth is estimated to be around $30 million as of 2023.

Piers Morgan Career Highlight

His career in journalism kicked off in 1985, working as a reporter for the South London News and the Streatham and Tooting News. He later became an editor for the News of the World in 1994, followed by a role as editor at the Daily Mirror from 1995 to 2004.

Piers Morgan Writing Career

Alongside his journalism, Morgan is an accomplished author. He has written over 11 books, including autobiographies, collections of columns, and biographies of notable personalities such as Simon Cowell and Cristiano Ronaldo.

Piers Morgan Television Career

In addition to his journalistic and authorial pursuits, Piers Morgan has established himself as a popular television personality, hosting or appearing in numerous shows, including “The Importance of Being Famous,” “Morgan and Platell,” “America’s Got Talent,” “Britain’s Got Talent,” and “The Dark Side of Fame with Piers Morgan.”

Piers Morgan Notable Achievements

Morgan has been honored with various awards and nominations for his work as a journalist and television personality. Notable accolades include the “Interviewer of the Year” award from the Royal Television Society in 2003 and a TRIC Award for Best News Presenter in 2021.

Piers Morgan Wife

Who is Piers Morgan’s Wife? Piers Morgan has been married twice. His first marriage was to Marion Shalloe in 1991, with whom he had three sons named Albert, Spencer, and Stanley. Following their divorce in 2008, Piers married journalist Celia Walden in 2010, with whom he shares a daughter named Elise.

Piers Morgan Children

Piers Morgan has four children – three sons named Albert, Spencer, and Stanley, from his first marriage, and a daughter named Elise with his second wife, Celia Walden.
