Lil Wayne, Young Jeezy Add 10 Dates To Tour

Fans of Lil Wayne and Young Jeezy can rejoyce, as the “Young Money Presents: The America’s Most Wanted Music Festival” has been extended by 10 shows.

The tour, which also features Soulja Boy and Jeremih, in addition to Pleasure P and the Young Money crew, will continue through September.

It hasn’t been all good news for the tour, however, as headliner Drake suffered an onstage leg injury, and had to pull out of the tour [click to read]. Drake did, however, appear during the tour’s set in Toronto on Tuesday (Aug. 4).

Here are the additional tour dates for the “America’s Most Wanted” tour:

8/24 – Kansas City, MO @ Starlight Theatre
8/27 – Darien Center, NY @ Darien Lake Theme Park Resort
8/28 – Noblesville, IN @ Verizon Wireless Music Center
8/29 – Tinley Park, IL @ First Midwest Bank Amphitheatre
8/30 – Clarkston, MI @ DTE Energy Music Theatre
9/01 – St. Paul, MN @ Xcel Energy Center
9/02 – Milwaukee, WI @ Marcus Amphitheatre
9/03 – Cincinnati, OH @ PNC Pavilion at Riverbend
9/04 – Charlotte, NC @ Verizon Wireless Amphitheatre
9/06 – Sunrise, FL @ BankAtlantic Center

The show’s next stop is at the Blossom Music Center in Cuyahoga Falls, OH.
