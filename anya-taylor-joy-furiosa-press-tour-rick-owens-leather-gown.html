Anya Taylor-Joy Looks Like a Fantasy in a &quot;Furiosa&quot;-esque Leather Dress

As one fashionable press tour ends, another begins. So it was with Margot Robbie’s Barbie-core red-carpet run, and then Zendaya’s shifts for Dune: Part Two and Challengers. Now, the baton passes to Anya Taylor-Joy, entering the spotlight ahead of the release of Mad Max prequel Furiosa with sartorial aplomb.

Alongside costar Chris Hemsworth, Taylor-Joy attended a photo call in Sydney, Australia, today dressed to the nines—thanks to an incredible one-shoulder gown plucked straight from Rick Owens’s Spring 2024 collection.

Brendon Thorne//Getty Images

The dress featured a draped asymmetric top the color of burnt umber, primly tucked into a high-rise trumpet leather skirt with a train awash in a rusty red. The gown perfectly embodied the dystopian desert vibes emulated throughout the Mad Max franchise (a fact perhaps made clearer by the shirtless, makeup-clad gang of dancers posing behind the star?).

Related Stories

As an ambassador for Tiffany & Co., Taylor-Joy repped the luxury brand with a few major jewelry pieces, from a glistening gold arm cuff to diamond-embellished chain earrings.

Brendon Thorne//Getty Images

When Taylor-Joy first touched down in Sydney (the Mad Max franchise is set and filmed Down Under), she soft-launched her Furiosa press tour wardrobe with an airport fit that leaned a bit more casual. This consisted of a unique sleeveless turtleneck blouse, with the top half featuring ribbed knit and the bottom half featuring a billowing see-through mesh. She styled this with gray twill pleated trousers from Aritzia, a black pair of Maison Margiela’s leather Tabi loafers, and black oval sunglasses from Dior.
