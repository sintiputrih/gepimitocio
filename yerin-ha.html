Meet Halo Actress Yerin Ha Wiki | Bio

  • 25 years old Yerin Ha (born in 1997) is an Australia-Korean Actress who has been floating in the spotlight since she joined the cast of Paramount’s famous Video game-based series “Halo.”
  • Paramount Sci-fi series “Halo” is her life’s first mammoth big break in Which Dune Casting Director Jina Jay gave her the chance to perform the role of “Quan Ah” in 9 episodes.
  • In “Halo,” Yerin got the chance to land after responding to an executive producer Steven Spielberg on a Facebook casting call.

  • Before shooting started, she did a lot of like physical training initially, but at one point, she realized that her character Quan has a lot of emotions.
  • To connect with her character “Quan Ah” physically, emotionally, and mentally, Yerin Ha talked with directors and producers even did handy textbook work, which really helped her.
  • Before landing in “Halo,” Yerin made her fabulous appearance in the portrayal of “Ah Rah” in Australian Producer Karl Zwicky’s series “Troppo” on February 27, 2022.
  • In 2022, where on the one hand, she is gaining some fame for performing in the series “Tropo,” shortly after, her appearance as “Tracey” in the movie “Sissy” increased her prominence.

Early Life & Family & Training

  • The 5 feet 2 inches tall actress Yerin Ha was born in 1997 to her South Korean parents in Sydney City of Australia, but she didn’t reveal her Birthday date yet.
  • She found a passion for performing from a very young age because her grandmother is an actor, and her parents met each other in an acting school as per Vogue.
  • Growing up around a family who loved the arts, Ha was naturally attracted to the side of her performing arts; that’s why at the age of 15, she joined Kaywon Performing Arts High School in Seoul, South Korea.

  • From 2013 to 2015, while studying at “Kaywon Academy,”  she learned for herself what it meant to be an Asian actress, and this realization increased her visibility in the Asian arts.
  • After graduating from “Kaywon,” Yerin moved back to Sydney, where she enrolled in the National Institute of Dramatic Art (NIDA) in 2016.

Acting Journey

  • While Studying at the “National Institute of Dramatic Art,” Yerin Ha began her acting journey as a theater artist by performing in “Caucasian Chalk Circle” & “The Changeling” Plays in 2017.
  • From 2017 to 2018, she participated in “I Walk In Your Words,” “Women on the Verge of a Nervous Breakdown,” & “The Colby Sisters of Pittsburgh Pennsylvania.”
  • After graduating in 2018, Ha joined the cast of “Lord of the Flies” in a Sydney Theatre Company production in which she got the chance to work with actress Mia Wasikowska.

  • On the big screen, she made her first appearance as “Techie Jane” in the 2019 released series “Reef Break,” after that, she did “Troppo” “Sissy,” & “Halo,” and nowadays Director Corrie Chen is casting in mini-series “Bad Behaviour.”
  • In addition to acting, Yerin is also a dancer with over ten years of experience in various genres from contemporary and jazz to tap.
