Emily Ratajkowski Stays Cool in a Green Crop Top and Cutoff Shorts

Let Emily Ratajkowski’s dog-walking fashion streak continue.

The model stepped out for a stroll with her dog, Colombo, in New York City on Thursday, in a fresh summer look composed of a light green sleeveless ribbed crop top and a pair of black denim cutoff shorts—leaving her abs on display. She cinched the high-waisted shorts at the waist with a black leather belt and finished the ensemble with white-and-gray cowboy boots by Montserrat Messeguer. She accessorized with her go-to brown oval sunglasses.

EmRata let her red hair flow straight down and seemed to wear minimal makeup and a long beige manicure.

BACKGRID

Ratajkowski regularly shows off her experimental street style around the city.

Last month, she debuted a string of head-turning looks, including a vibrant minidress she wore while walking her pup. It was a skintight pink minidress with red lace and a bold red rose print—the vibrant piece from Guizio perfectly matching her fiery new hair color. She wore the mesh mini with white-and-pink Puma Palermo sneakers and elevated the look with a sleek brown crossbody bag by Loewe, oversize black square sunglasses, big gold hoops, and her gold Sylvester necklace.

On another occasion, she stepped out with Colombo while wearing a slinky cropped white halter top, brown wide-leg snakeskin-print pants, and purple-and-blue sneakers. She accessorized with a black leather woven shoulder bag, large black square sunglasses, and gold jewelry, including big hoop earrings and dainty necklaces.
