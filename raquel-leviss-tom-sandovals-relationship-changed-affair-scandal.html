Raquel Leviss And Tom Sandovals Relationship Radically Changed After Affair Scandal

Highlights

  • Raquel Leviss and Tom Sandoval's scandalous affair took center stage on Vanderpump Rules season 10.
  • Raquel initially claimed she was in love with Tom during their affair.
  • Tom Sandoval and Raquel Leviss are no longer together.

Raquel Leviss and Tom Sandoval's scandalous affair became the highlight of Vanderpump Rules season 10 just as the season was wrapping up. They became the rave of the moment, albeit in a bad light. While the duo went head-to-head with Sandoval's longtime partner, Ariana Madix, and their other cast mates, they put up a united front to prove they wanted to be together.

The TV stars made headlines for months as their clandestine affair unraveled before the Bravo show's fans. However, what seemed to be a solid relationship borne out of cheating fizzled out as fast as it was exposed. When Tom Sandoval and Raquel Leviss' affair drama died down, it appeared their romance went with it. Here's a look at where the pair stand concerning their relationship after Scandoval.

Are Tom Sandoval And Raquel Leviss Still Together After Their Vanderpump Rules Affair?

After weeks of appearing in the news for their cheating scandal, Tom Sandoval and Raquel Leviss' affair was dissected at the Vanderpump Rules three-part reunion. During this time, Leviss opened up about the actual timeline of their hidden relationship, including how she and Sandoval were intimate in the house he shared with longtime partner Ariana Madix.

The Vanderpump Rules stars delved into details of their relationship with Raquel Leviss, appearing to make the most reveals. When Andy Coen asked the TV star about the motive behind her affair with Tom Sandoval, she noted, "I did it because Tom and I had this connection." Leviss relayed that they drew closer to each other because he made her feel ''heard." When asked if she loved him, the reality star quietly admitted that she did. Sandoval also returned the favor when he was asked.

With these confessions, it is safe to say that Vanderpump Rules fans were convinced the pair wanted to be together. Raquel Leviss maintained that she had feelings for Sandoval even through the most explosive confessions. However, in August 2023, she backtracked during a sit-down with Bethenny Frankel on her ReWives podcast. When the host asked if she loved him, Leviss said,

"I believed I was in love with Tom at the time. I now look back on it and see that I was not in love with him. I also don't believe he truly was in love with me either."
