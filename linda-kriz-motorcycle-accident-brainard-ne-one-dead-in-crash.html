Linda Kriz Obituary, Death – After an accident that took place early on Tuesday morning close to the intersection of Casa Linda Drive and Norton Street, a young man who was riding a motorcycle was sent to a local hospital as a precaution. The accident took place close to the intersection of Casa Linda Drive and Norton Street. The accident took place as a result of a collision between the child and a pickup truck.

During the rush hour traffic that occurred before to the beginning of classes at Baker Middle School, the reports that the cars were moving in the incorrect direction were brought to the attention of the police. According to the statements made by the authorities, the individual who was driving the pickup truck at the time of the incident that resulted in the collision was in the process of driving two children to their respective schools when the incident occurred.

The motorcyclist, on the other hand, was heading in the complete opposite direction. The truck driver was attempting to make a left turn into Casa Linda when the incident took place. The driver allegedly had their eyes covered, and as a result, they were unable to see the rider who was in the road. This information comes from the police.

Despite the fact that it appeared to the police that the motorcyclist had not sustained any significant injuries as a result of the accident, the officer decided to transport the rider to the hospital just in case. His mother, Linda Caldera, claimed that her son sustained a concussion as a result of the incident, as stated in claims that were made by Linda Caldera in an interview with KRIS 6 News.
