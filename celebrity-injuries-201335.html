Celebrity Injuries Through the Years: Photos

Down but not out. Reese Witherspoon, Teddi Mellencamp and more celebrities have bounced back from minor injuries over the years.

The Legally Blonde actress ended up with a gash on her forehead in September 2011 after she was struck by a car while on a run in Los Angeles.

“The driver seemed to have not seen her because of a large tree at the intersection,” an eyewitness exclusively told Us Weekly at the time. “[Reese] was lucky, because it could have been much more serious.”

The Hello Sunshine founder “suffered minor injuries,” according to the police report, and was transported to a local hospital for treatment. Witherspoon was later released from the hospital and went home to recover, while the 84-year-old driver was cited for failing to yield to a pedestrian in a crosswalk.

Nearly a decade later, The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills alum Mellencamp shared photos from her own injury.

“I gave 2020 the boot — and 2021 returned the favor!” the former Bravo personality joked in January 2021 via social media alongside a photo of her on crutches. Mellencamp twisted her ankle while playing pickleball with her family over the holidays.

She made light of the situation, telling her followers, “The silver lining: it’s called a ‘dancer’s fracture’ so I’m basically a ballerina now. Pickle Ball: 1. Teddi: 0.”

Mariska Hargitay, for her part, suffered two separate injuries in 2021. She announced in May of that year that she discovered after getting an MRI that she had a broken knee, a hairline fracture in her ankle and a torn ligament.

“Great news is I don’t need surgery🙏🏻 💃🏻 #YouShouldSeeTheOtherGuy😜,” she playfully captioned an Instagram snap after leaving the doctors. “Thanks for all the love folks! Just so you know this did NoT happen at work!”
