Jaimie Alexander Bikini Body, Peter Facinelli Shirtless: Beach PDA Pic

Nothing like a good ole’ getaway! Peter Facinelli and his girlfriend Jaimie Alexander were all smiles as they vacationed in Nuevo Vallarta, Mexico during the Thanksgiving holidays. The couple arrived on Thanksgiving day and stayed at Villa La Estancia Riviera Nayarit. While swimming in the ocean, Facinelli, 40, wore short bathing suit trunks and showed off his tight abs. The 29-year-old Thor actress, meanwhile, wore a tiny bikini with her brunette locks slicked back in a low bun. Alexander later was seen grinning ear-to-ear as Facinelli carried her on his back in the pool. (Facinelli turned the big 4-0 during the trip on Nov. 26.)

The pair clearly took full advantage of their trip together. A source tells Us that the actors also received couples massages at the resort’s Tatewari Spa and jet-skied around Banderas Bay.
