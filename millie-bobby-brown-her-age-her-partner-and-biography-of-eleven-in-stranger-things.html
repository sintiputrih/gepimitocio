MILLIE BOBBY BROWN: HER AGE, HER PARTNER AND BIOGRAPHY OF ELEVEN IN STRANGER THINGS

‘Stranger Things’ has established itself as one of the most successful and popular fictions on all of Netflix since it came to light with its first season. Now, in the middle of 2022 and with its fourth installment just out of the oven, the series has returned to our screens to reveal to viewers the new threats that threaten the young Hawkins gang. Little protagonists , not so little anymore, where the actress Millie Bobby Brown stands out .

The artist gives life to the character of Eleven in the plot of ‘Stranger Things’ and throughout the seasons she has shown that age is only a number, and that this does not prevent her from having great acting talent. In fact, in recent years there have been several productions that she has joined, putting herself under the skin of other characters.

For this reason, we will carry out a small investigation in order to get to know a little more about the actress of Spanish origin who is causing a global sensation. A very professional young woman with a very established professional career that she has not left anyone indifferent. You’ll be surprised!

YOUR PROFESSIONAL CAREER

Born on February 19, 2004 in Marbella, Spain, Millie Bobby Brown is an actress and model who moved with her family to Bournemouth (Dorset) when she was only four years old, a country where she was not too long either, since, four years later he emigrated to Orlando (Florida).

Her connection to the world of acting came at a very young age, as her father enrolled her in a weekend school where Millie developed her acting, dancing and singing skills. It was there that an agent of hers met her talent and suggested that she should try her luck in Hollywood . Her first professional project came in 2013, when she was cast in the ABC fantasy drama series ‘Once Upon a Time in Wonderland’, playing the role of young Alice.

In 2015, Brown played Lizzie on the popular series ‘Modern Family,’ and later appeared on ‘Grey’s Anatomy’ as Ruby. Small roles on screen that led her to return from Spain a year later, but it was then that she found out that she was selected for the main cast of the series that would change her life forever, ‘Stranger Things’. Since then, the young woman’s professional career has not stopped growing, even making the leap to the big screen.

Among his most recent films stands out ‘Godzilla vs. Kong ‘, the two ‘Enola Holmes’ feature films, where she has also made her first steps as a producer, or ‘Godzilla: King of the Monsters’. On the other hand, she has also been the special guest of video clips of great music stars such as Drake, ‘In My Feelings’, Mariah Carey, ‘All I Want for Christmas Is You’ (2019), or Maroon 5 and Cardi B in ‘Girls Like You’.

All this added to the numerous awards with which it has been awarded. Some of her most notable awards are a Saturn Award for ‘outstanding young actress in a television series’, and she has been nominated for both Emmy and Screen Actors Guild Awards for the role of Ella Ella Eleven she. In addition, she is one of the youngest actresses to have been nominated in the history of the Emmy Awards and to have been named a UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador.

HIS PERSONAL LIFE

On a personal level, Millie Bobby Brown is shown to be a faithful follower of social networks. In fact, on her Instagram account she has almost 50 million followers, a platform where she also shares her closest and most intimate face. In addition, she has shown that, not counting her interpretation, she is very passionate about fashion. 

And it is that Millie Bobby Brown has been the promotional image for several advertising campaigns and has been on the cover of several magazines. A facet where she has made it clear that she feels like a fish in water. On the other hand, in the field of love, the actress had her first romantic relationship in 2017, when she was 13 years old, with the young singer Jacob Sartorius. This love story ended in 2018, and it was not until 2020 that it became known that the actress had been with the rugby player Joseph Robinson.

Another of the celebrities who conquered her heart was Romeo Beckham, the son of David and Victoria Beckham, but their romance did not last long. Currently, the 18-year-old actress proudly boasts her new partner, Jacob Hurley Bongiovi , son of the well-known singer Jon Bon Jovi.
