Who Is Chelsea Handler Baby Daddy?

As of July 1, 2023, there is no available information regarding Chelsea Handler having a baby. Therefore, she doesn’t have a baby daddy.

However, in 2016, the American comedian disclosed in an essay featured in Playboy magazine that she underwent two abortions at 16.

Even though she was pretty young, Chelsea initially considered keeping the baby.

But, upon confessing the situation to her parents, they promptly took her to Planned Parenthood for further assistance.

The TV presenter said, “I felt parented, ironically, while I was getting an abortion. And when it was over, I was relieved in every possible way.”

Furthermore, the actress and producer have decided not to be a parent and enter into motherhood as of now.

Also, in a conversation with Piers Morgan a few years back, Handler was stunned when the former CNN host expressed his belief that she would excel as a mother.

She replied to Morgan, “Well, that’s sweet of you, but you wouldn’t know what kind of mother I would be.”

Moreover, Handler clarified that if she were to have a child, many other aspects of her life would be compromised. Hence, she is happy being an aunt to her niece and nephew.

Chelsea Handler dating history

Host and comedian Chelsea Handler have had multiple romantic relationships in the past.

The comedian entered into a relationship with Ted Harbert in 2006. Ted was CEO at Comcast Entertainment Group, overseeing E! and other cable channels during that time.

However, after being in a relationship for four years, they ended their connection in 2010 due to the challenges of mixing business with pleasure.

Shortly after her breakup with Harbert, she started seeing Animal Planet star Dave Salmoni.

Their relationship lasted only a few months, but one of the most memorable aspects of their time together was when Chelsea openly discussed the breakup on her talk show.

Following that relationship, Handler engaged in a brief fling with rapper 50 Cent.

Although the television host and the rapper didn’t commit to each other, they shared a genuine bond and didn’t hold back grudges against each other.

Chelsea then subsequently entered into an on-and-off relationship with hotelier André Balazs.

Sadly 2013, Handler revealed in one of her talk show episodes that she and André had decided to end their relationship.

And eventually, she was romantically involved with Jo Koy from 2021 to 2022.

Despite Koy’s frequent appearances as a stand-up comic on Chelsea’s E! late-night series Chelsea Lately, their romantic relationship began several years later.

Speculation about their relationship started in September 2021 when Chelsea and Jo attended a Dodgers game together.

Confirming their status, the actress publicly acknowledged their relationship via her Instagram.

However, on July 18, 2022, Chelsea and Jo announced their separation, informing their fans about the end of their relationship.
