Laura Wasser’s Career

Laura Wasser began her career as an attorney after graduating from Loyola Law School. She joined her father’s law firm, Wasser, Cooperman & Mandles, which was established in 1976. Her expertise in family law, particularly divorce cases, has made her a sought-after attorney in Hollywood.

Author and Entrepreneur
