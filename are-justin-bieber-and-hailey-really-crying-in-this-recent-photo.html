Are Justin Bieber And Hailey Really Crying In This Recent Photo?

They're one of the world's most famous couples and are ever ready to serve major PDA. Justin and Hailey Bieber have been married for over two years and they've had their ups and downs, but the relationship between the it-couple has always been strong.

The couple recently traveled back to the Bahamas, their favorite vacation spot, and were seen spending time together. A fan captured a picture of the married couple, and it seems like the two of them are having some troubles...or not?

Trouble In Paradise For The Biebers?

Justin and Hailey love each other very dearly and make sure to share that on social media now and then. So when a fan captured a photo of them in the Bahamas, in an awkward moment, it has left others wondering if the Biebers were having an argument.

Instagram Gossip account DeuxMoi first shared the photo, which sees Hailey and Justin dressed in casual attire, seated at a restaurant table. Hailey is seen resting her head on her knee, while Justin is seen resting his head on the table.

While some fans think the couple was "avoiding being photographed," others said "Crying or praying. It could go either way."

One fan was concerned about the future of their marriage, and shared "I hope everything is ok. I actually like them as a couple."

"I smell divorce.." another user wrote, and another replied "they look stressed."

One user defended the couple, sharing "They may be tired. And sheesh what is peace can’t even unwind without folks snapping pictures..."

It's quite possible that the Biebers had a really long day, and wanted to unwind in silence instead of being disturbed or bothered by the paparazzi.
