What Is Kanye Wests Favorite Color?

Ever wondered what Kanye West’s favorite color is?

Kanye West doesn’t have a particular favorite color, but based on his dressing he seems to like black the most. He enjoys neutral colors in general, and he hates the color blue with a passion!

Read on to learn more!

Kanye Seems to Enjoy Black and Neutral Colors 

Kanye’s clothing has certain predominant elements to it – and the one that stands out the most is his love for the color black. While Kanye has never spoken about black being his favorite, we can make a safe assumption here. Other elements he uses in his outfits include oversized coats and skinny jeans.  

Besides black, Kanye likes to wear neutral and muted colors like gray, army green, coffee khaki, and washed blue. He uses denim often, and he likes to layer it too. Layering is an important part of his wardrobe, and he isn’t afraid to wear denim on denim either. His outfits are generally aimed at creating a low-key sense of leisure, except for events where he means to make a statement.

Kanye has an amazing fashion sense, so it is no surprise that he has ventured into the world of high fashion. His fashion line is called Yeezy, and it debuted at the New York Fashion Week of Fall 2015. Kanye sees this line as “the Hermès of sportswear.” The collection tends to be monochromatic in grays, browns, blacks, and even some oranges – and the fits tend to be extremes; either skin-tight or loose enough for the body to be swallowed completely. 

But the most famous part of the Yeezy empire is the sneakers. Take for instance the iconic Yeezy Boost 350, which was made in collaboration with Adidas Originals. The sneakers were an instant hit, and they came in gray. The price soon hit four figures after hundreds of fans swarmed to Adidas stores to get their hands on a pair. The shoes have since turned into a pop-cultural sensation, and have been seen on the feet of celebrities like Pharrell Williams and Gigi Hadid. 

Kanye Simply Cannot Stand Blue 

While most celebrities tend to be a bit conservative in interviews and try not to reveal too much about themselves, keeping their opinions to the absolute bare minimum, Kanye is almost always enthusiastic in the strangest ways and goes into detail about the strangest things. In an interview he spoke about how he hates the color blue with a passion, saying, “I don’t use blue. I don’t like it. It bugs me out. I hate it.”

Even though Yeezy is Kanye most famous fashion venture, his biggest fashion achievement might just be his infamous transformation of Kim Kardashian West’s style after he began dating her, and which became apparent in 2012.

Kanye was able to swap Kim’s early 2000s aesthetic – bright makeup and voluminous hair complimenting low-rise skinny jeans and leggings, mid-calf length boots, and body con dresses from – for a more modern aesthetic – a subtler and more contoured makeup style, sleek hair, and monochromatic and minimalist outfits. He introduced Kim to more timeless brands like Givenchy, Versace, Balmain, and Azzedine Alaïa.
