Is Misty Copeland Pregnant With Husband Olu Evans? Her Kids &amp; Net Worth Details

Is Misty Copeland pregnant with her husband, Olu Evans? There is a rumor on the internet about the American ballet dancer’s pregnancy.

Misty Copeland is a famous ballet dancer at American Ballet Theatre.

Misty is the ballet dancer for one of the three leading classical ballet companies in the United States.

Copeland is the first-ever African-American as the principal dancer for the American Ballet Theatre.

Following success as a dancer, the 39-year-old is also a public speaker, celebrity spokesperson, and stage performer.

The Kansa City native became a member of the ABT’s in 2000 and an ABT soloist in 2007.

In 2015, The stunning ballerina was named 100 most influential people globally.

To date, she has appeared on many music videos, television series, and stage shows, including her debut with the documentary A Ballerina’s Tale, a documentary film about Copeland.
