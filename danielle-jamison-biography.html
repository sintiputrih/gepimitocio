Who is Danielle Jamison?

Danielle Jamison is a popular reality television star, actress, and model. She is better known as, a receptionist at 9Mag tattoo shop and a celeb for the “Black Ink Crew: Chicago” which aired on VH1.

It represents the daily functions and drama at a tattoo shop owned and operated by African-Ameican. The tattoo studio is present in Chicago, Illinois. The seventh season of the show was aired on the 4th of October, 2021.
