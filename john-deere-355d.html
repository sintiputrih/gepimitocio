John Deere 355D Specs, Price, Review, Attachments 2022

John Deere 355D Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Weight, Serial Numbers,  Attachments, Features & Pictures

John Deere 355D Garden Tractor is equipped with a Yanmar 2V78 diesel 2-cylinder liquid-cooled vertical-shaft V-twin 749 cc engine, which delivers 18 hp (13.4 kW) power.

John Deere 355D Garden Tractor featured with a 18HP Yanmar 749cc 2-cyl diesel engine, hydrostatic transmission, 3.8 gal fuel tank capacity, 4×2 2WD, wet disc brakes, 709 lbs weight, infinite forward and reverse gears and 4.7 qts 4.4 L oil capacity.

John Deere 355D Garden tractor attachments include 54″ mid-mount mower deck, 48″ mid-mount mower deck, 44″ mid-mount mower deck, 38″ mid-mount mower deck, snowblower and blade.
