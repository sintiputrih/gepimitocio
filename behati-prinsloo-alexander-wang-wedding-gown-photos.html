See Photos of Behati Prinsloo's Cutout Wedding Reception Dress

In July 2014, Victoria's Secret Angel Behati Prinsloo tied the knot with Maroon 5 front man Adam Levine. The pair wed in a romantic ceremony at Flora Farms in Los Cabos, Mexico, with Prinsloo wearing a custom wedding gown by Marchesa. However, Prinsloo and Levine have remained relatively tight-lipped about their nuptials. Until now, that is.

Paying tribute to designer Alexander Wang on his birthday, Prinsloo shared throwback photos of the bespoke bridal gown he made in 2014 for her evening wedding reception. Sleek, white, and party-ready, the gown features revealing sheer panels on the torso and down both sides, and as expected, is a jaw-dropper.

Sharing two snaps taken during her wedding dress fitting in June 2014, Prinsloo tagged Wang and revealed, "This dress you made me for my wedding, I'm still not over it haha!"

It was actually Instagram's "Post a Picture Of ..." trend that ignited her desire to share more details about her wedding. Sharing the most recent picture saved on her phone, the model revealed that she'd tried on her Alexander Wang reception gown once more, captioning the picture, "Still fits."

Instagram

She also shared, "Adam ripped it so I could dance better," and pointed out the gown's torn seam.

Fans also asked Prinsloo to post an unseen photo of her pregnancy belly, the first picture she could find of her with Levine, and her favorite moment from the couple's 2014 nuptials. She obliged and posted a series of beautiful photos on her Instagram Stories, featuring a plethora of special memories.
