Fake Empire | Lyrics, Song Meanings &amp; Music Videos

Fake Empire are a Portsmouth, UK based post-punk band formed in 2017 and have released nine singles with 'Elders', their latest single, being released in January 2019. The band's current lineup consists of Les Black, Becky Bellinger and Simon Brookes.

2. Fake Empire is an indie-rock band that formed at the University of New Hampshire in 2007. The band's initial lineup consisted of Anthony Terlizzi (guitar/vocals) and Brian Leahy (guitar), and Emily Ranauro (vocals) joined shortly thereafter.

In the fall of 2009 bassist Dan Weatherford and drummer Joe Tonelli joined the band.

Fake Empire has produced several recordings in the last three years with the Boston-based producer Paul McCaffrey, including "Firefly", "On The Run", and "Offshore".
