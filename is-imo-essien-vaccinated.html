Is Imo Essien vaccinated?

Imo Essien is known to be a basketball player who was known to have collapsed during one of his playing hours for the Old Dominion against Georgia Southern. Though much information is not known about him, Imo Essien was known to have joined the team before the start of the 2021-2022 season with an average performance of 7.1 minutes per game.

Yes. Imo Essien was given a SADS (sudden adult death syndrome) vaccination which was known to have caused his collapse.
