Alessandra Ambrosio Shows Off Impressive Abs in Black Sports Bra Post-Gym

This Angel is kicking her fitness routine into high gear! With the annual Victoria's Secret Fashion Show just around the corner in Paris, Alessandra Ambrosio is making exercise one of her top priorities.

Ambrosio pulled her long brunette strands back into a wet bun.
