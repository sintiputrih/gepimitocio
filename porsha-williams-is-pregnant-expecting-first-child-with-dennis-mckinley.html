Porsha Williams Is Pregnant, Expecting First Child With Dennis McKinley

Porsha Williams is rocking a new baby bump! The Real Housewives of Atlanta star is expecting her first child with her boyfriend, Dennis McKinley, Us Weekly can confirm.

After teasing her new love to listeners on her Dish Nation radio show, Williams, 37, debuted her relationship with McKinley, 38, on social media in June.

“🎉Screaming Happy Birthday to my love #DM❤️ Let’s make it last foreverrrr.🎼,” she captioned a series of pictures and videos of the twosome. “Yes we have a secret handshake lol he matches my goofy swagg😆 #Swipe.”

More recently, the Bravo star shared a sweet selfie of herself and the entrepreneur on vacation in Boca Raton, Florida. She captioned the post with two clown emojis and a heart emoji on Sunday, September 2. Two days later, Williams and her RHOA castmate Shamea Morton went on a double date with their beaus at the Atlanta Braves game.

“Out with one our fav couples @shameamorton & Gerald 💏💏 #Braves#Baseball ⚾️,” Williams wrote on Tuesday, September 4, alongside a series of photos from the baseball game of herself, McKinley, Morton and her husband, Gerald Mwangi.

Williams was previously married to former NFL star Kordell Stewart. The duo were married for two years before they split in 2013. After their divorce was finalized, the reality TV personality briefly reconnected with her ex-boyfriend Todd Stewart. 

In March 2017, Williams opened up about learning to love again after her first marriage ended.
