Where is The Price is Right filmed?

FOR OVER 50 seasons, The Price is Right was filmed in Television City at Bob Barker's Studio 33.

In 2023, the beloved game show moved locations, and now fans want to know where the new studio is.

Where is The Price is Right filmed?

For 51 seasons, The Price is Right was filmed at Bob Barker Studio 33 in Los Angeles, California, which was named after the show's original host.

Despite the legacy of the studio, the show decided to move to Haven Studios in Glendale, which is approximately 10 miles away.

Haven Studios is also the home of Let's Make a Deal.

Why did the Price is Right move to another studio?

The Emmy Award-winning program's change in filming locations is attributed to Television City's billion-dollar renovations.

The renovations at the Hollywood landmark are expected to expand its capacity from eight production studios to 15.

Television City is also home to Dancing With the Stars, The Young and The Restless, and American Idol.

On June 26, 2023, The Price is Right shot its last episode at Studio 33.

"Today we say farewell to the legendary Bob Barker Studio," the game show told its fans via Instagram.

"Thank you for all the memories. We’ll see everybody in Glendale next season!"

In another Instagram post, Drew Carey also shared: "We’ve been here for 51 years in this historic stage.

"I used to tell people there was magic in the wood here because of all the good vibes that have been in this studio.

"You know, all the laughs, and all the fun that have been here.

"Now, we got to go and start a new place, put our own good vibes into that new place."

He added: "I am blessed by being able to walk the same stage that people like Bob Barker (and) Carol Burnett used to walk on.

"Honestly, it’s a real blessing in my life. I always am grateful and thankful that I have this opportunity to have a job like this."

Taping at Haven Studios began on July 28, 2023.

How can I watch The Price is Right?

Every Monday through Friday, The Price is Right is broadcasted at 11am EST on CBS.

Sometime in September 2023, season 52 is expected to start airing at the abovementioned time slot.
