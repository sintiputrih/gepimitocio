Is Evan Ross Related To Terrence Howard? Everything To Know

Terrence Howard and Evan Ross are not related to each other, but Howard introduced Ross as his nephew on Celebrity Family Feud. Here’s what we know about their relationship.

Terrence Howard is an American actor who came into the spotlight after landing a prominent role in 2005’s Hustle & Flow earned him an Academy Award nomination. Meanwhile, his first major roles came in the 1995 films Dead Presidents and Mr. Holland’s Opus; he broke into the mainstream with a series of television and cinema roles between 2004 and 2006.

Apart from that, we can see his notable works in Hustle & Flow, Crash, Iron Man, and Four Brothers. Besides acting, Howard has also worked as a producer and writer. His producing works can be seen in Triumph, Terrence Howard’s Fright Club, and The Ledge.

Is Terrence Howard Related To Evan Ross?

Terrence Howard and Evan Ross are not related to each other, and they do not have any blood relationship. Meanwhile, they both are engaged in the same field and have gained massive names and fame with their amazing acting.

For your information, Ross is one of the most loved American actors who made his acting debut in the comedy-drama film ATL (2006). Since then, he has had an active presence in this field and has given numerous hit movies and tv series.

Are Terrence Howard and Evan Ross Siblings or Nephews?

Once, actor Terrence Howard introduced Evan Ross as his nephew on Celebrity Family Fued. Since then, their fans have remained curious to know about their relationship. According to people, they are not related to each other.

And the same report has also said that Howard is a close family friend of the Ross family. Talking about the family background of Ross, he was born to Arne Næss Jr and Diana Ross. He also has one older full brother named Ross Næss.

On the other hand, Howard was born to Tyrone and Anita. According to a source, his siblings are Tyrone V Howard Jr., Antonio Howard, and Terrelle Howard. And his half-siblings are Arriana Michelle & Darnell Williams.

Terrence Howard Wife Name

Terrence Howard is married to his beloved wife, Mira Pak. Pak is his third wife. The pair had a back-and-forth relationship in their married life as they separated at some point in their life and then again got remarried.

The duo tied the knot in 2013, and after two years, they got separated. However, after three years, they again got engaged in 2018 and then remarried. Apart from that, Howard’s first wife is Lori McCommas, who died in an automobile accident in 1993.

Michelle Ghent is his second wife, with whom he was together from 2010 to 2013. From his multiple marriages, Howard has five children named Heaven Howard, Hunter Howard, Qirin Love Howard, Hero Howard, and Aubrey Howard.
