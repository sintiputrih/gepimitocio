Brent Rivera Bio: Age, Height, Sister, Movies, Net Worth &amp; Pictures

Brent Austin Rivera well known as Brent Rivera is an American actor and social media personality born on January 9, 1998.

Brent was born in Huntington Beach, California, U.S.

His father, John Rivera is also former Vine star.

He has two brothers, Brice and Blake and a sister named Lexi.

He is a Vine star turned YouTube star. He was formerly known as MrBrent98 on YouTube.

Rivera has a height of 5 feet 11 inches and weight is 61 kg.

Career

Brent Rivera started acting at the age of 11.

He became popular on Vine where he has over 8 million followers and has been able to earned over 12 million followers on YouTube.

Prior to fame, he had created his YouTube channel in 2009 and his Vine channel in 2013.

He also became known for his 2018 video “Wearing Long Acrylic Nails for 24 hours” featuring Eva Gutovski. The video has over 10 million views.

Rivera also started hosting a web series “Dream Vacation” in 2019.

As an actor, he is known for his roles as Isaac Salcedo in the movie “Light as a feather” and Alex in “Alexander IRL”

He has worked with the likes of Alan Stokes, Lexi Hensler, Ben Azelart and so on.

Personal Life
