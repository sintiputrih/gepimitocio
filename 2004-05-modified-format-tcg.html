From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

The 2004-05 Modified format of the Pokémon Trading Card Game (informally called RS-on and now referred to as Standard format) was a former Standard format used for Play! Pokémon organized play and in most sanctioned tournaments worldwide from September 2004 to August 2005.
