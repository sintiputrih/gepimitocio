Ayra Starr Drops Visuals For Latest Single, Rush

Raving Nigerian singer and performing artiste, Oyinkansola Sarah Aderibigbe, better known by her stage name, Ayra Starr has released visuals to her recent output, ‘Rush‘.

As we gear up towards the release of ’19 & Dangerous Deluxe Edition’, the Mavin Records prodigy has blessed the screens with a colorful and an eye pleasing video to her chart topping single, ‘Rush’.

‘Rush’ which was released on Friday September 16, 2022 debuted on Apple Music Charts across the globe duting its first week of release and has peaked at the No. 1 position of Apple Music Nigeria.
