NLE Choppa Disses The Concept Of OnlyFans

NLE Choppa is let his opinions fly on Sunday (April 19). The Memphis-bred upstart hopped on Twitter to criticize people who subscribe to OnlyFans, a paid-subscription platform that’s become largely associated with the adult entertainment industry.

“Porn is free how tf y’all making these hoes rich off only fans,” he tweeted.

Choppa later clarified his comments weren’t directed at the platform’s content creators but rather those who are subscribers.
