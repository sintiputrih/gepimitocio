Who Is Asia Argento Boyfriend, Michele Titi Martignoni?

Being an MMA fighter has its perks, and one of them is certainly being the ladies’ man. Just take a look at the Italian thunder, Michele Titi Martignoni, who stole the heart of the actress and filmmaker, Asia Argento, the millionaire Anthony Bourdain’s ex-girlfriend.

Get to know Michele better as you scroll down.

Meet Michele Titi Martignoni, Asia Argento New Boyfriend

Michele Titi Martignoni and Asia Argento made their relationship IG official on August 21, 2022. 

“When you wake up in the morning, remember what a precious privilege it is to be alive: to breathe, to think, to feel joy, and to love – Marcus Aurelius,” the MMA fighter captioned his IG post flaunting a series of lovey-dovey pictures.

Asia then graced the comment-section writing, “Your kiss is like a rock (kiss emoji).”

However, though Michele only revealed Asia on his IG on August 21, the actress had already featured him on her IG on August 17. Over the pics, the two were vacationing at the sea, sharing smiles and hugs.

Prior to Asia, Michele dated the aspiring model, Yasmin @yasminels.

The two were together until 2021.

Michele once even featured his ex-girlfriend on his IG thanking her for everything she’s ever done for him. “Thank you my love for everything you have organized for me. Your closeness day after day gives me a great desire to win, and believe me I already have a lot of mine… with you (friends and fans) by my side I don’t stop more,” the caption read.

Asia Argento Rela Love Life Before Michele Titi Martignoni

Before Michele, Asia   Argento dated the late Anthony Bourdain who was 20 years older than her. The two began their romance in 2016 after meeting on the set of the CNN show Parts Unknown. However, their relationship wasn’t the one for the books. 

According to the actress, they both repeatedly cheated on one another and were both “ok” with it. “It wasn’t a problem for us. He was a man who traveled 265 days a year. We took great pleasure in each other’s company when we saw each other. But we are not children. We are grown-ups,” she explained.

Sadly, Anthony committed suicide in June 2018, right after the photos of Asia and French reporter Hugo Clement cozying up with each other surfaced online. Naturally, some claimed that Asia had murdered her boyfriend. To which, the actress explained, “People need to think that he killed himself for something like this? He had cheated on me too. It wasn’t a problem for us.”

Furthermore, Asia added that she was infact angry at Anthony for leaving “abandoning (her and her) kid.”

For those who don’t know, Asia has two children —  Anna Lou (born in 2001 to Asia and her former partner, Italian musician Marco Castoldi), and Nicola Giovanni (born in 2008 to Asia and the film director Michele Civetta).

Sadly, Anna — who was named after Asia’s half-sister — tragically died in a motorcycle accident at the age of 21.

Michele Titi Martignoni Family

Michele Titi Martignoni comes from a family of five — his parents, himself, and his two siblings.

His father, Marco Martignoni @dr.marcomartignoni is a Boston University Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine graduate who went on work as a dentist at Studio Martignoni s.r.l. He was in a domestic partnership with Gaia Straccamore (a theater actress) in 2022.

As for Michele’s siblings, his sister Maria Sole Martignoni @m___sole is an Istituto Santa Giuliana Falconieri graduate. While his brother, Matteo Martignoni is an MMA fighter like him.

Matteo turned 21 on October 23, 2021.

The Martignoni brothers often trained with each other. No wonder, they were both skilled.

Trivia: Michele’s grandfather is named Paolo Zincone @paolozincone.

Michele Titi Martignoni Job

Michele Titi Martignoni is a rising MMA star. At the time of writing this article, he had a record of 7 wins and 1 loss (against Nathan Fletcher).

So, how did a son of a dentist become an MMA fighter? Well, Michele originally joined MMA as a teenager to increase his self-confidence. Then he started competing in Italian championships and emerged victorious twice. So, once he decided that he had accumulated enough experience, he transited to professionalism.

But even though he’s a professional now, he still has different coaches for each discipline. The last we checked, he trained with Riccardo Carfagna and Lorenzo Borgomeo. 

The Italian thunder, Michele got his nickname after breaking the record of the fastest victory in the history of the discipline (in just 7 seconds with a flying kick).

Did you know: Michele spent 48 hours in the emergency room at the Tor Vergata Polyclinic in June 2020.
