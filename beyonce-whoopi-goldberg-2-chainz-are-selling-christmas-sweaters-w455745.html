Beyonce, Whoopi Goldberg, 2 Chainz Are Selling Christmas Sweaters

Nothing ugly about these cozy toppers! With December 25 right around the corner — and Ugly Sweater Day December 13 — the ugly sweater obsession is in full swing, and celebs like Beyoncé, Whoopi Goldberg and 2 Chainz are ready to feed your (perfectly healthy) habit.

Making the most of her banner year — and ear-worm lyrics — Queen Bey, 35, unleashed a candy apple-red sweater emblazoned with a yuletide spin on her motto: “I Sleigh All Day” ($60, shop.beyonce.com). Meanwhile, Goldberg, 61, teamed up with Lord & Taylor for a luxe line of pullovers, like the Whoopi Goldberg Zip-Up Ugly Christmas Sweater featuring both a black and a white Santa going head-to-head ($139, lordandtaylor.com).

One of our favorites, though, has to be 2 Chainz’s line of tops. After breaking the Internet with his dabbing-Santa sweaters last year, the “Watch Out” rapper, 39, is back with Santas that “juju on the beat” and “hit dem folks.” All of his sweaters are currently on sale for $35, with a portion of the proceeds going to families in need and Chainz’s own TRU Foundation.

As for his dancing Santas? 2 Chainz tells Stylish, “I’m really seeking inspiration from the kids, what are they into and what excites them.”
