Insane 200k Lamborghini that's one of 'rarest and coolest' in the world spotted parked on London s

An incredible Lamborghini worth more than £200,000 that's "one of the rarest and coolest" available has been spotted parked down an unassuming London street. A user filmed the luxury car and shared a video to TikTok where fans gushed over the pricey Lamborghini.

TikTok user Supercarseurope shared a video of the white car with a caption that said: "One of Lamborghini's rarest and coolest cars tucked away down a side street." The user added: "The things you find street-parked in London."

The impressive car, worth around £230,000 according to classic.com has a green, white and red stripe down the middle to look like the Italian flag. Some TikTok users gushed over the car in the comments, while others were divided over the design.

One TikTok user commented: "Perfect car, sv has it all." Another said: "Haven't seen this one in years." Other users were less impressed, as one said: "What a spec but those black rims are… [sick face emojji]." Another commented: "Not a fan of the stripe."

Car fans turned into online detectives recently when a Rolls Royce covered in what appeared to be diamonds was spotted. TikTokers from London were also certain they had seen the car roaming around the capital.

One user wrote: "I saw the same one (checked license plate from a video) like 4 months ago in London and back then only the roof was covered." Another penned underneath: "I have seen that for in London irl".

The number plate Dar IIa sparked plenty of speculation about who might own it. In fact the Rolls Royce is owned by Daria Radionova, founder of DCouture in Knightsbridge.

As for the Lamborghini, the number plate is simply '7 LDN' but it's unclear who owns it.
