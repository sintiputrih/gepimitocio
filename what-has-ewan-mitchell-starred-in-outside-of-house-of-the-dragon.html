What Has Ewan Mitchell Starred In Outside Of House Of The Dragon?

Thanks to the many time jumps in season one of House of the Dragon we get to see several different actors play certain characters. Fans of the Game of Thrones prequel have seen some characters go from birth to adulthood even before the season ended. One such character is the prince Aemond Targaryen. In episode eight, English actor, Ewan Mitchell makes his House of the Dragon debut as an adult Aemond. The younger version of the character was played by Leo Ashton over the two previous episodes.Fans of House of the Dragon are so far loving Ewan Mitchell’s portrayal of Aemond Targaryen, who they likened to his uncle Prince Daemon Targaryen, another fan favorite, a role played by Matt Smith. House of the Dragon may have introduced Ewan Mitchell to many new fans, but he’s been around for a while. Mitchell’s on screen debut came back in 2015, in the form of two short films, Stereotype and Fire. House of the Dragon might be his biggest project to date, and It is likely that this will push his career to the next level, judging by the response on social media. One person wrote on twitter, “Whoever casted Ewan Mitchell as Aemond Targaryen, give them a raise immediately. #HouseOfTheDragon.”The series is moving quite fast, one can only hope we get enough time to see Ewan Mitchell in this role. 8 Ewan Mitchell Starred In Trigger Point This Year

Earlier this year Ewan Mitchell appeared in three episodes of the British crime/drama series Trigger Point. The series follows a Lana Washington (Vicky McClure), an experienced bomb disposal officer working for the Metropolitan Police. Mitchell played Billy Washington, Lana's younger brother, who tried several times to join the British Army, but failed. In addition to his chronic unemployment, he had difficulty maintaining a social life.

7 Ewan Mitchell Guest Starred In This Medical Soap Opera In 2017

Ewan Mitchell also guest starred in an episode of BBC’s medical soap opera, Doctors (2017). In the episode, he plays Genyen, a young Buddhist monk collecting in the street. This was just one of four roles played by Mitchell in 2017.

6 Ewan Mitchell Also Starred In This Series Alongside James Norton In 2017

In 2017, Mitchell guest starred on an episode of Grantchester alongside James Norton (Little Women), Robson Green (Casualty), and Al Weaver (Colette). The series is about a Cambridgeshire clergyman who finds himself investigating a series of mysterious wrongdoings in his small village of Grantchester.

5 Evan Mitchell Also Played This Small Role In 2017

Ewan Mitchell played a small role in the 2017 movie, Just Charlie. The film is about Charlie, a football (soccer) star who is a girl trapped in the body of a boy. After revealing her secret, Charlie is rejected by her teammates and her father who hopes to see Charlie join a big football team. The movie has a perfect score on Rotten Tomatoes from just seven critic reviews.

4 Ewan Mitchell Starred In This Downton Abbey-esque Series

If you are a fan of Downton Abbey, then you might also like The Halcyon (2017), in which Ewan Mitchell played Billy Taylor, a bellboy at the Halcyon Hotel. The ITV series that also featured stars like Mark Benton (The Second Coming) and Jamie Blackley (Becoming Elizabeth), was canceled by the network after the first season.

3 Ewan Mitchell Took On This Role In PBS’ Historical Drama Series

He played Tom Bennett in the first season of PBS’ World On Fire. The series is a WWII drama focused on the lives of ordinary people affected by the war. The second season is currently in production, however we might have to wait to see if Evan Mitchell will reprise his role.

2 This Is One Of Ewan Mitchell’s Most Notable Roles

His portrayal of Ettore in High Life is one of Ewan Mitchell’s most notable roles. He starred in the 2018 movie alongside Twilight heartthrob, Robert Pattinson, and rapper André 3000. High Life is a story of a father and his daughter’s struggle to survive in deep space where they live in isolation.

1 Ewan Mitchell Starred In This Netflix Hit

Ewan Mitchell played Osferth in the British historical fiction series The Last Kingdom, which aired its final season on Netflix in March 2022. The actor took on this career defining role back in 2017. The series that has 91% average rating on Rotten Tomatoes, is based on Bernard Cornwell’s The Saxon Stories series of novels.
