How much is Spencer Rattler making in NIL?

NIL, which stands for name, image, and likeness, refers to the ability of college athletes to monetize their personal brand by allowing them to profit from their name, image, and likeness without jeopardizing their eligibility to play college sports. The NCAA announced the NIL rules in July 2021, and since then, college athletes have been signing endorsement deals, selling merchandise, and making appearances to earn money from their personal brand.

Spencer Rattler, the starting quarterback for the University of Oklahoma Sooners, is among the college athletes who are likely to benefit significantly from the new NIL rules.
