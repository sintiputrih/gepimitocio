Resident Evil 4 Remake Release Date &amp; Time

The Resident Evil 4 remake release date and time is almost upon us, and fans can’t wait to sink their teeth into it. So, the question on many a mind is going to be: “when is the RE4 remake coming out?” Well, that’s exactly what we’ll be discussing in this guide, with all the information that we have. So, let’s dive straight in.

When is Resident Evil 4 Remake Coming Out

The Resident Evil 4 remake is coming out on March 23rd, but the exact release date and time will probably vary depending on your timezone and platform. As a reminder, the new RE4 will launch on Xbox Series X/S, Xbox One, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5 and PC. There’s no official info on when exactly you’ll be able to start playing on each platform, so we’ll have to engage in some educated guesswork. First off, the countdown on the official Steam page should be more than enough information for PC players. It seems to indicate that the game will release on midnight March 24th, which probably means that we’re looking at a rolling midnight launch. To eliminate any possibility of error, just check the countdown and you’ll be golden.
