Kyle Trask- Biography

Kyle Trask is an American football player who currently plays for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League. His position on the team is as a Quarterback. He previously attended the University of Florida and played collegiate football there. In 2020, he received the Earl Campbell Tyler Rose Award and was also recognized as the FBS passing touchdown leader.

Kyle Trask- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Kyle was born on March 6, 1998, in Manvel, Texas, in the United States of America. His parents’ names are Michael Trask and Melissa Charba. He was named after the football stadium at Texas A&M University. He was always interested in athletics when he was a child.

Kyle finished his high school studies at Manvel High School in Texas. He attended the University of Florida for further and higher education. He was a college football player over there.
