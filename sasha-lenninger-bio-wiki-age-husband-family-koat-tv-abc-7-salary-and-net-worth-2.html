Sasha Lenninger Career

Sasha Lenninger KOAT-TV: ABC17

Sasha was first hired as a live reporter and producer, then promoted to co-host and promoted again to weekend anchor. She was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, and graduated Sua Cum Laude from the Walter Cronkite School of Journalism at Arizona State University.

Sasha enjoyed internships with Arizona’s main newspaper, The Arizona Republic as a breaking news writer, KPNX Channel 12 News, assisting in finding stories, shooting stories, and writing scripts while also assisting in producing Channel 12’s EVB Live show. She is currently interning with KNPX Arizona Midday, assisting in production, scriptwriting, designing graphics as well as studio production.

Sasha has volunteered with the Special Olympics as a basketball coach as well as with the HoopStars program, a basketball league for disabled individuals in Southern California. Sasha holds a black belt in karate and has extensive travel experience in South East Asia as well as Europe.
