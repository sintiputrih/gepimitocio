About Consulate General of France in Los Angeles, United States

French Consulate in Los Angeles runs an inclusive range of consular services to local, French, and international citizens in United-states.

The French Consulate in Los Angeles supports American citizens through its consular services, if they:

  • Need general or specific info regarding French economy, culture, sports, education and more
  • Need information regarding requirements and process of getting French Citizenship
  • Specific contacts and information in France
  • Want to apply and obtain French Visa

Visa and Passport services

French passport and visa from are issued only to citizens whose main residence is in France and United-states.

The process of issuing French Visa and Passport of France can take few weeks.

*Please contact with French Consulate in Los Angeles if you have any question as regards of visa and passport matters.
