Route 124 (Japanese: １２４番水道 Water Route 124) is a water route in northeast Hoenn, connecting Lilycove City, Mossdeep City and Route 126. This route is inaccessible until the player defeats Team MagmaROR/Team AquaSEAS in Lilycove City.

Route description

The main path of the route begins as an east–west route from Lilycove, widening as it progresses east. Near the center of the route, the path turns to the south. At the mouth of the outlet near Mossdeep, a pair of islands play home to the Treasure Hunter, who will exchange Shards for Evolution stones. South of the outlet, Route 124 becomes Route 126.

There are 16 spots to Dive along the route. Although not every spot is connected to one another via underwater pathways, most are, allowing Trainers to reach otherwise inaccessible areas of Route 124. Seaweed, the underwater equivalent of tall grass, line the submarine passages of the route.

Items

All items on Route 124 can only be accessed with the use of Surf, except for those traded for at the Treasure Hunter's house, which may be accessed via Fly or Soar.

All items found underwater necessarily require Surf and Dive.

Appearance

In the manga

Pokémon Adventures

Omega Ruby & Alpha Sapphire arc

Route 124 first appeared in PS610, where it was revealed that the rocket launched from the Mossdeep Space Center in an attempt to stop the meteoroid headed for Hoenn had crashed down there due to Zinnia disrupting its flight. She had then proceeded to destroy the crucial dimensional shifter component, dooming the Devon Corporation's attempt at stopping the meteoroid.

In PS612, the Hoenn Frontier Brains succeeded in neutralizing the Admins of Neo Team Aqua and Neo Team Magma near the crashed rocket.
