Ciara is an American singer, songwriter, record producer, dancer, fashion model, and actor. She released her debut studio album, Goodies, on September 28, 2004, and it was certified ‘3x Platinum’ by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) and garnered 2 nominations at the 48th Annual Grammy Awards, while also establishing Ciara as a teen idol. Her discography also includes other albums and songs like Dance like We’re Making Love, Ciara: The Evolution, Jackie, Beauty Marks, Ciara, Fantasy Ride, I Bet, Basic Instinct, Thinkin Bout You, Greatest Love, Can’t Leave ’em Alone (ft. 50 Cent), Speechless, Sorry, and Evapora (with Iza and Major Lazer). In addition to that, she has also worked in several shows and movies including Mama, I Want to Sing!, All You’ve Got, America’s Next Top Model, America’s Most Musical Family, All That, That’s My Boy, I Can Do That, The Game, Idols South Africa, and Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn.
