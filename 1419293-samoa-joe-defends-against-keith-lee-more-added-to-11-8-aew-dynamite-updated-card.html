Samoa Joe Defends Against Keith Lee &amp; More Added To 11/8 AEW Dynamite, Updated Card

Next week’s episode of AEW Dynamite is set to be a big one, and will feature multiple title matches, as well as a big ROH World TV Championship bout.

After confirming some matches during AEW Rampage, a handful of other matches were confirmed for the show during tonight’s episode of AEW Collision, including a stellar match between Keith Lee and Samoa Joe, with Joe defending his title against the giant superstar.

Elsewhere in the night, Penta El Zero Miedo will take on Swerve Strickland in a singles match, after Alex Abrahantes issued the challenge on behalf of Penta.

Jay White and Mark Briscoe will also face off in the ring, with Briscoe getting a shot at the AEW World Championship at AEW Full Gear if he comes away with a win. Julia Hart will also face off against Red Velvet, with Hart returning to the ring for the first time in a month.

For the fully updated card for the November 8 episode of AEW Dynamite, check out below:

  • MJF (c) vs. Daniel Garcia
  • Penta El Zero Miedo vs. Swerve Strickland
  • Jay White vs. Mark Briscoe
  • Red Velvet vs. Julia Hart
  • Samoa Joe (c) vs. Keith Lee
